**Persepolis: The Ancient Heart of Persia**

## متن

Persepolis, which is located near Shiraz, is one of the most impressive historical sites in Iran. This ancient city was founded by Darius the Great nearly 2,500 years ago. The ruins, which include tall columns and detailed stone carvings, show the advanced architecture of the Achaemenid Empire.  
Persepolis was a ceremonial capital, where kings held grand celebrations called “Nowruz.” Visitors who walk among these stones can imagine the glory of ancient Persia.  
Today, Persepolis attracts tourists from all over the world who want to experience the rich cultural heritage of Iran. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of the nation’s history.

**Naqsh-e Jahan Square: The Grand Heart of Isfahan**

## متن  

Naqsh-e Jahan Square, which is located in the center of Isfahan, is one of the largest historical squares in the world. It was built in the early 17th century during the Safavid dynasty. Around the square, there are important buildings such as the Shah Mosque, Sheikh Lotfollah Mosque, and Ali Qapu Palace, which all show the beauty of Persian-Islamic architecture.  

In the past, the square was a place where kings held ceremonies, watched polo games, and met foreign ambassadors. Today, it is a lively tourist attraction where visitors can walk, take photos, and enjoy traditional shops and tea houses. Naqsh-e Jahan is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that represents the cultural heritage of Isfahan and Iran.

Si-o-se-pol Bridge: The Iconic River Passage of Isfahan

### متن  
Si-o-se-pol, which is also known as the Bridge of 33 Arches, is one of the most famous bridges in Isfahan. It was built in the early 17th century during the Safavid dynasty and crosses the Zayandeh River. The bridge, which is made of brick and stone, is a beautiful example of Persian architecture and engineering.  

In the past, Si-o-se-pol was not only a bridge, but also a place where people gathered to walk, talk, and enjoy the view of the river. At night, the bridge created a magical atmosphere when its arches reflected in the water. Today, it is still a popular tourist attraction where locals and visitors sit, drink tea, and listen to the sound of the river.

## متن ۲ – Computer / تخصصی  
### عنوان متن  
Load Balancers: Distributing Traffic in Modern Web Systems

### متن  
In modern web applications, a load balancer is a crucial component which distributes incoming traffic across multiple servers. Instead of sending all requests to a single machine, the load balancer acts like a smart gateway that decides where each request should go. This helps prevent any server from becoming overloaded, which could slow down the system or even cause downtime.  

There are different types of load balancers, such as layer 4 and layer 7 load balancers, which make routing decisions based on various information. For example, a layer 7 load balancer can inspect the URL or headers and then direct the request to a specific service. In cloud environments, load balancing allows systems to scale horizontally, so new servers can be added when traffic increases and removed when it goes down.

### عنوان متن  
Golestan Palace: The Royal Garden of Tehran

### متن  

Golestan Palace, which is located in the historic center of Tehran, is one of the oldest royal complexes in Iran. Most of its present buildings were constructed during the Qajar dynasty, which ruled the country from the late 18th to the early 20th century. The complex includes several halls, museums, and gardens that display a combination of traditional Persian art and European influences.  

Many important coronation ceremonies took place in Golestan Palace, where kings received foreign ambassadors and high-ranking officials. The mirrored halls and colorful tilework create a unique atmosphere that reflects the luxury of the Qajar court. Today, the palace is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular tourist attraction that represents the cultural and political history of Tehran.

## متن ۲ – Computer / تخصصی  
### عنوان متن  
Microservices Architecture: Breaking Applications into Smaller Services

### متن  

Microservices architecture is a design approach in which a large application is built as a collection of small, independent services. Each service focuses on a specific business capability, such as user authentication, payment processing, or notification delivery. These services communicate with each other through lightweight protocols, often using HTTP or message queues.  

One of the main advantages of microservices is that teams can deploy and scale each service independently, which increases flexibility and resilience. If one service fails, it does not necessarily bring down the entire system. However, microservices also introduce challenges, such as more complex monitoring, distributed debugging, and the need for reliable service discovery and API gateways.
